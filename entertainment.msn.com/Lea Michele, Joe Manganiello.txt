Lea Michele, Joe Manganiello, Abigail Breslin & Keke Palmer Join Fox’s ‘Scream Queens’

The long-rumored Glee reunion is a reality. Series star Lea Michele is set to co-star inScream Queens, the followup Fox series from Glee creators Ryan Murphy, Brad Falchuk and Ian Brennan. Also joining the cast are Joe Manganiello (True Blood), Abigail Breslin (Little Miss Sunshine) and Keke Palmer (Akeela and the Bee). Additionally, Ariana Grande will have a recurring role.

Jamie Lee Curtis and Emma Roberts were previously cast in starring roles. Murphy, Brad Falchuk, Ian Brennan and Dante Di Loreto executive produce for 20th Century Fox TV.

The first installment in the new anthology series revolves around a college campus which is rocked by a series of murders. Fifteen one-hour episodes have been ordered, which are scheduled to begin production in the spring, and premiere in fall 2015 on Fox.
